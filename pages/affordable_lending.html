Affordable Lending
Platform for Sustainable Product Pipeline

This project helped streamline and scale a national affordable lending program by establishing a deal "toolkit" for team members to reference, increasing efficiencies in the deal execution process.

The toolkit included deal checklists, standardized processes, execution tools and artifacts, and well-defined roles and responsibilities to ensure consistency, efficiency, and alignment across the team.

Role: Management Consultant

The Outcomes

Streamlined
Execution Process

After analyzing the execution process, tools were created to fill the gaps and reduce manual labor needed to close deals in a timely manner. This helped reduce uncertainty and provided clarity to the team, enhancing overall efficiency.

Standardized
Processes and Artifacts

The standardization of key processes and artifacts ensured consistency and compliance with contract requirements. Documented roles and responsibilities, detailed guidelines, and standard templates provided clear references for team members, driving uniformity in execution.

Improved
Scalability

The new streamlined process and clear documentation equipped the client to handle a higher volume of deals without sacrificing efficiency or quality. This enhanced scalability supported the successful construction and rehabilitation of affordable housing projects.

The Process

The development of this affordable lending platform involved several key steps to ensure a streamlined and scalable solution for a top national multifamily lender.

Thorough Analysis and Planning

The project began with a thorough analysis of the lending market and the client's existing processes. A detailed roadmap was created to identify key milestones and deliverables, ensuring a clear path forward.

Development and Integration

The toolkit was developed and integrated into the client's operations. This included creating the Deal Execution Checklist, Tools, and Processes and Artifacts, all designed to improve consistency and efficiency in the deal execution process.

Training and Implementation

Team members were trained on the new toolkit, ensuring they were equipped to utilize the new processes and tools effectively. This phase focused on embedding the new practices into the daily operations of the team.

Continuous Monitoring and Evaluation

After implementation, continuous monitoring was established to track the performance of the new processes. Regular evaluations were conducted to ensure ongoing efficiency and identify areas for further improvement.

Deployment and Scaling

With the new processes and tools in place, the client was ready to scale their operations. The platform supported an increased volume of deals, ensuring consistency and quality as the program expanded.
